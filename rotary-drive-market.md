<p><p>The rotary drive market encompasses three primary types: electric, pneumatic, and hydraulic rotary drives. Electric rotary drives utilize electrical energy to produce rotational motion, offering high precision and efficiency. Pneumatic rotary drives harness compressed air for movement, ideal for applications requiring quick response times and lightweight systems. Hydraulic rotary drives operate using pressurized hydraulic fluid, providing significant torque and load capacity, making them suitable for heavy-duty industrial applications. Each type serves distinct industry needs based on performance and application requirements.</p></p>
