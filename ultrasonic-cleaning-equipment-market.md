<p><ul><li>Magnetostrictive</li><li>Piezoelectric</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Ultrasonic cleaning equipment utilizes sound waves to remove contaminants from various surfaces. The market can be divided into two main types: magnetostrictive and piezoelectric. Magnetostrictive systems use a magnetostrictive transducer to generate ultrasonic waves, offering high efficiency and durability, making them ideal for industrial applications. In contrast, piezoelectric systems employ piezoelectric crystals that vibrate when an electric charge is applied, providing more compact designs suitable for smaller cleaning tasks, such as jewelry and delicate electronic components.</p></p>
